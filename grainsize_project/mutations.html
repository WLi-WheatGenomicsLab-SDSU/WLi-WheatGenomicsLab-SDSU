Grain Size Project

Sequence information of new TaCKX2-A1, TaCKX2-B1 and TaCKX2-D1 mutations in the T2 progeny plants from event #4-6. The mutation types include deletions (dashed line), insertions (lower-case letters in black) and substitutions (blue letters). PAM sequences are in green. Black dots mean nucleotides not shown.
